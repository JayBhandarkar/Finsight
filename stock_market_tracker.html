Stock Market Tracker

Real-time stock market monitoring with predictive insights and personalized alerts for better investment decisions.

How to Use

  1. Start typing a company name (e.g., "Apple" or "Microsoft")
  2. Select a company from the suggestions
  3. Click "Add Stock" or press Enter
  4. Track multiple stocks at once
  5. Prices update automatically every minute
  6. Click "Remove" to stop tracking a stock

Features of Stock Market Tracker

Real-Time Tracking

Monitor stock prices with automatic updates every minute, ensuring you have access to the most current market data.

Global Markets

Track stocks from various markets around the world, with support for multiple exchanges and currencies.

Visual Indicators

Color-coded price changes and animations make it easy to identify market trends at a glance.

Secure Data

All market data is fetched securely using industry-standard APIs, ensuring reliability and accuracy.
